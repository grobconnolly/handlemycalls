Why are we building this?

As a small business owner, I would receive 10-20 solicitation calls a day. From business funding calls, service provider, to even IRS spam calls. I finally had enough and decided to build a tool that would stop all the unwanted calls. When I developed this tool, all of my unwanted calls stopped. At the same time I noticed my productivity increased, my business performed better, and I had more time to spend with my family.
